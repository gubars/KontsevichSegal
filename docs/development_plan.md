# Development Plan

## Phase 1: Complex Metrics (Section 2)
Define the space QC(V) of allowable complex metrics on a real vector space V.
Prove the diagonal characterization (Theorem 2.2) and the angle condition |arg(g_ii)| < π/2.
Establish contractibility and the domain of holomorphy structure.
Prove restriction to subspaces (Proposition 2.5).

## Phase 2: Cobordism Category (Section 3)
Define the category of Riemannian cobordisms and its complexification.
Construct the semigroup structure on amplitudes.

## Phase 3: Field Theory Axioms as Structure (Section 3)
Formalize the Kontsevich-Segal axioms as a Lean structure/typeclass.
Define the partition function Z and its functorial properties.

## Phase 4: Unitarity and Wick Rotation (Sections 3 and 5)
State and prove the unitarity condition on the boundary of QC(V).
Formalize the Wick rotation as a limiting process from complex to Lorentzian metrics.
